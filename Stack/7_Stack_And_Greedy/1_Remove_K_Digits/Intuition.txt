Question Link : https://leetcode.com/problems/remove-k-digits/description/

*   Problem:
        Remove k digits to form smallest number

*   Example:
        "1432219", k=3 → "1219"

*   Key difficulty:

        Choosing which digits to remove

*   Core idea:

        GREEDY + MONOTONIC STACK

*   Key intuition:

        If left digit > right digit
        → remove left digit

*   Why?

        Smaller digit at front → smaller number

*   Strategy:

        Maintain increasing stack

        For each digit:
            while top > current AND k > 0:
                remove top

*   After traversal:

        If k still > 0:
            remove from end

*   Why end?

        Remaining digits are increasing
        → tail has largest digits

*   Important detail:

        Skip leading zeros

*   Time Complexity:

        O(n)

*   Space Complexity:

        O(n)
