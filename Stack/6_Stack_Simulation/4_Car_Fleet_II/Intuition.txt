Question Link : https://leetcode.com/problems/car-fleet-ii/description/

*   Problem:
        Each car may collide with next car

        After collision → forms fleet
        speed becomes equal

*   Goal:
        Find FIRST collision time for each car

*   Key difficulty:

        Chain collisions

        Car ahead may change behavior BEFORE we reach it

*   Core idea:

        Use MONOTONIC STACK from right → left

*   Why right → left?

        Because collisions depend on cars ahead

*   Stack meaning:

        Stores candidate cars to collide with

*   Removal conditions:

        1. Cannot catch up:
            speed[i] <= speed[j]

        2. Front car collides earlier:
            time(i→j) > res[j]

            → j changes before we reach

*   Compute time:

        time = (pos[j] - pos[i]) / (speed[i] - speed[j])

*   Important invariant:

        Stack only contains VALID future targets

*   Time Complexity:

        O(n)

*   Space Complexity:

        O(n)
