Question Link : https://leetcode.com/problems/maximum-subarray-min-product/description/

*   Problem:
        Maximize:
            (minimum of subarray) * (sum of subarray)

*   Key difficulty:

        Both MIN and SUM involved

*   Brute force:

        Try all subarrays
        → O(n^2)

*   Core idea:

        Treat each element as MINIMUM

*   Why?

        For each nums[i]:
            find maximum range where it is minimum

*   Use monotonic stack:

        To find:
            nearest smaller to left (NSL)
            nearest smaller to right (NSR)

*   Range:

        (NSL+1) → (NSR-1)

*   Key insight:

        This range gives MAX sum
        where nums[i] remains minimum

*   Why maximum?

        Because expanding range increases sum
        and nums[i] is still minimum

*   Need prefix sum:

        To compute subarray sum in O(1)

*   Formula:

        sum = prefix[right] - prefix[left]

*   Final computation:

        For each i:
            sum * nums[i]

*   Take maximum

*   Important invariant:

        nums[i] is minimum in its valid range

*   Time Complexity:

        O(n)

*   Space Complexity:

        O(n)
