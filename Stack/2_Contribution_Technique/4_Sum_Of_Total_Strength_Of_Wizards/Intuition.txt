Question Link : https://leetcode.com/problems/sum-of-total-strength-of-wizards/description/

*   Problem:
        Sum of total strength of all subarrays

        total strength = min(subarray) * sum(subarray)

*   Key difficulty:

        Both:
            minimum
            AND sum of subarray

*   Brute force:

        For each subarray:
            compute min and sum

        → O(n^2) → too slow

*   Core idea:

        Treat each element as MINIMUM

*   Step 1:

        Find range where element is minimum

        Using monotonic stack:
            left[i]  = nearest smaller left
            right[i] = nearest smaller right

*   Step 2:

        For each i:
            calculate sum of all subarrays
            where i is minimum

*   Key challenge:

        Efficiently compute:
            sum of ALL subarray sums

*   Trick:

        Use DOUBLE PREFIX SUM

*   Why double prefix?

        Single prefix:
            gives sum of one subarray

        Double prefix:
            gives sum of MANY subarrays

*   Formula idea:

        Split into:
            left part contribution
            right part contribution

*   Final formula:

        contribution =
            (rightSum * leftCount)
            - (leftSum * rightCount)

*   Multiply by strength[i]

*   Important invariant:

        Each element contributes
        only in range where it is minimum

*   Time Complexity:

        O(n)

*   Space Complexity:

        O(n)
